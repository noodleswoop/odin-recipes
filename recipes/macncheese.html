Quick, Essential Stovetop Mac-and-Cheese

Description

frankly i have yet to make this but it looks perfect for a lazy person like yours truly

Ingredients

  • Kosher salt
  • 4 oz dried pasta, such as macaroni or another small twisty shape
  • 2 tsp salted or unsalted butter
  • 2 tsp all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Many grinds of black pepper
  • 1/2 cup finely grated parmesan or pecorino cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a small-to-medium pot of very well-salted water to a boil and add your dried pasta. Cook it until firm and tender, then drain.
  2. Return pot to stove and melt butter in the bottom. Using a spoon or whisk, add flour and mix until it disappears.
  3. Add milk, a tiny splash at a time, stirring constantly so no lumps form.
  4. Season with 1/4 tsp kosher salt and many grinds of black pepper.
  5. Bring sauce to a simmer. Cook, stirring, for 1 to 2 minutes.
  6. Remove from heat and stir in grated cheese until combined.
  7. Add drained pasta, stir evenly to coat.
  8. Scoop into a bowl and finish it with more black pepper if you wish. Repeat as needed.
